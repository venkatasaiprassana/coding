About my Amazing Daddy

        "When people look inside my life, I want to hear them say

         She’s got her father’s eyes

         Her father’s eyes

       Eyes that find the good in things

       When good is not around”

My father is my hero and guide in my life. He is the one I look upon whenever I find myself in trouble.
My father has been my guiding force for all my major decisions in life.
In fact, I have never regretted adhering to his advice as it has always worked for me.
He is a hardworking and passionate person. My father has a love for movies.
Whenever he gets time, he just loves to watch an old movie
In fact, at times we just fight as to who gets control of the television.
But at heart, we love to tease each other and play as well.

A role model is a person who I look up to and have greatly influenced in my life
and that special person is my father.
He possesses certain qualities of a good role model that set examples of being comical, supportive,
and shows great strengths of a hard worker.
Being comical through hardships is one of a kind trait that my father holds.
He is just full of joy and happiness that nothing stops him for being too gay.
Seeing him every day and night happily makes my teeth crackles smiling.
He'll have his struggles, his complications, his hardships, but he fights with a positive and comical attitude.
In example, he was terminated from his job for attending a court session and he was glad that they let him go.
Most people mourned for their jobs, but my father was happy. I gave him a glare look and said, "Okay."
"I find it eccentric, but he is my father and who am I to judge him?"
